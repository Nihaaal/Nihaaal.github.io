COLLEGE VISION

To be a socioeconomic, cultural and educational institution, developing Sangam SKM College community to achieve our aspirations through education.

COLLEGE MISSION

To educate and inspire Sangam SKM College community to contribute to the development of a dynamic society through installing civic pride, cultural understanding and virtues of life-long learning, thus developing. national peace and prosperity.

COLLEGE VALUES

Safety : We assure safe and conductive environment for the teaching and learning process.

Creativity : We encourage the development of creative talents in our members.

Quality : We are committed to honour the rights and dignity of our members.

SANGAM SADHU KUPPUSWAMY MEMORIAL COLLEGE

Sangam SKM College believes in envisioning and shaping the lives of students through online learning. In the view of the COVID 19 Pandemic, this institution is optimistic in providing home based educational support to the students to ensure learning takes place during this extraordinary time. The aim is to ensure that students are occupied and engaged during this crisis as we need to stand in solidarity to protect our children. This online platform encourages students to get access to the educational worksheets and the school will continue to play its mandated role in educating and transforming students. The institution is optimistic to see the power of working collaboratively to solve the world's biggest challenge in curtailing the difference and understanding the urgency of educating students in this digital society. Hence, Sangam SKM College is trying to explore new possibilities with greater flexibility, resulting in potential benefits in accessibility to education for students across Nadi. While the country is facing the issue of Covid 19, the institution would like to encourage parental engagement and support to create a positive learning environment. This will enhance parents to share a responsibility to help and monitor their children learn and meet educational goals. Our website will allow strong collaboration of parents and teachers in improving children's technological skills, social skills, behaviour and adaptability. There fore we urge the parents and guardians to closely monitor their children's progress and guide them.
